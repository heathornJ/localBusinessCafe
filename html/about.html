About Us

We have lots to offer here at the Local Business Cafe!

Our Business

The Local Business Cafe is our pride and joy. The idea came about during an internship, where I'd run off and get the coffees for everyone. That's about all I did. So I thought "Why not get paid for these coffee runs?" I built the Local Business Cafe to be a great workspace for busy individuals. Strong coffee, tasty food, and WiFi strong enough for any business meeting!

Our Coffee

Tech has a massive impact on our local and global wildlife. The same can be said for coffee. It's estimated that one cup of coffee destroys roughly one square inch of rainforest, making it a leading cause of rainforest destruction. That's why all of our blends are Rainforest Alliance Certified.

Our Food

Caffeine isn't the only ingredient to a successful day of database management and business reports. We prepare both our meals and snacks fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients or have some of those local business prep for us themselves. By supporting us, you support the community too!
